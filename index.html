I am an undergraduate student in Computer Science and Technology at Lanzhou University, currently focusing on Agentic AI, AI for Science, large-model acceleration, graph learning, embodied intelligence, and medical image analysis.

I am currently a research intern at Tsinghua University, where I design a diffusion-based model to generate 3D electron density maps from PDB structures with controllable resolution. My recent work also covers multi-agent RAG, multimodal speculative decoding, graph unlearning, cryo-EM structure identification, and protein-RNA affinity prediction.

To date, I have been involved in papers submitted to or under review at Knowledge-Based Systems, Neurocomputing, Nature Machine Intelligence, and NeurIPS, together with 5 invention patents, 4 software copyrights, and multiple national and international competition awards.

Think Globally, Act Locally: A Hierarchical Multi-Agent Framework with Retrieval-Augmented Generation Yutong Wang, Yunjian Zhang, Yunrui Shang, Zirui Liu, Zijin Wu, Yina Cao, Zhongfeng Kang* Submitted to Knowledge-Based Systems in April 2026. SQB-Spec: Accelerating Large Multimodal Models through Task-Specific Speculative Decoding Jisheng Dang, Bimei Wang, Yizhou Zhang, Yunjian Zhang, Ning Lv, Hao Ye, Hong Peng, Juncheng Li, Bin Hu NeurIPS 2026 Conference Submission. GPRM-PRA: A Language Model and Geometric Deep Learning Based Framework for Accurate Protein-RNA Binding Affinity Prediction Yunjian Zhang, Yutong Wang, Rizhen Hu* Journal of Chemical Information and Modeling, With Editor.
